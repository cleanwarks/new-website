Kottayam is known as the land of letters, latex and lakes — and homes here reflect that heritage. From the large independent houses common along KK Road and in Pala, to the rubber estate properties of Kanjirappally and Erattupetta, Kottayam homes tend to be spacious, traditionally built and surrounded by lush greenery. That greenery, however, brings its own cleaning challenges: fallen leaves, green algae on floor tiles, pollen and the general buildup that Kerala's tropical vegetation produces year-round.

Clean Warks has been serving Kottayam homes since 2021. Our trained teams understand the specific maintenance needs of the district's homes — whether it's cleaning the mosaic floors of older Kerala houses, the laterite exteriors of traditional nalukettu homes, or the modern tile and granite surfaces of newer villas along the Kottayam bypass.
