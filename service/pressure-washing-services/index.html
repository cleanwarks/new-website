The Power of High-Pressure Cleaning

Some dirt doesn't respond to scrubbing and cleaning agents alone. Moss colonies embedded in tile grout, oil stains baked into a concrete driveway, algae on a compound wall, or years of atmospheric grime on a parking surface — these need the concentrated force of a high-pressure jet wash to dislodge and remove.

Clean Warks uses industrial pressure washing machines delivering water at up to 2,000–3,000 PSI — many times more powerful than a garden hose — combined with appropriate cleaning agents to achieve a genuinely clean result on outdoor hard surfaces. Our technicians are trained to use the right pressure setting and technique for each surface type, preventing damage to tiles, paintwork and mortar joints.

What We Pressure Wash

High-Pressure Jet Wash

Industrial-grade pressure washing equipment delivers powerful, targeted water jets that cut through years of embedded grime on any outdoor hard surface.

Moss & Algae Removal

Biological growth on tiles, concrete and walls is completely removed and treated with anti-algae solution to prevent rapid re-growth, especially after Kerala's monsoon season.

Stain Blasting

Oil, fuel and chemical stains on driveways and parking areas pre-treated with degreasers then jet washed — effective on even long-standing stains.

Driveway & Parking

Paved driveways, concrete car parks, multi-level parking surfaces and paving stone areas jet washed to restore original colour and slip-free texture.

Wall & Fence Washing

Compound walls, boundary fences, retaining walls and exterior building walls pressure washed to remove moss, staining and years of atmospheric deposit.

Safe for Tiles & Concrete

We use surface-appropriate pressure settings — lower PSI with wider angle nozzles for tiles to prevent joint damage, higher PSI for concrete and masonry surfaces.

Ideal Uses for Pressure Washing

Pressure washing is perfect for post-monsoon property refresh, pre-sale property preparation, pre-event cleanup (weddings, functions), commercial premises maintenance and industrial site cleaning. It's also the fastest way to clean large outdoor areas — a task that would take days with a scrubbing brush can often be completed in a few hours with pressure washing equipment.

Available across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore. We bring our own water supply for sites without convenient access. Contact us for large-area quotes.
