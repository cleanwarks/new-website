Why Your Sofa Needs Professional Cleaning

Your sofa is used every day, absorbing body oils, food spills, pet dander, dust mites and allergens deep within its fibres. Vacuuming removes surface dust but can't reach what's embedded in the fabric. Over time, even a sofa that looks clean can harbour bacteria, mould spores and allergens that affect your indoor air quality and family's health.

Clean Warks uses hot water extraction (steam cleaning) combined with upholstery shampoo to penetrate deep into sofa fibres, loosen embedded dirt and stains, and extract everything — leaving your sofa genuinely clean, deodorised and dry in just a few hours.

What's Included in Every Sofa Clean

Hot Steam Extraction

High-temperature steam is injected into the upholstery under pressure, dislodging dirt, bacteria and allergens from deep within the fabric before they're vacuumed out.

Stain Treatment

Individual stains pre-treated with targeted stain removers before the main clean — effective on food, drink, ink, grease and biological stains.

Pet Hair Removal

Specialist rubber tools and strong suction used to remove pet hair embedded in fabric — including in seams, cushion edges and under cushions.

Fabric & Leather Safe

We assess your sofa material before starting — different products and methods are used for fabric, microfibre, velvet and genuine or faux leather.

Odour Elimination

Enzymatic deodorisers applied after cleaning to neutralise pet, food and musty odours at the source — not just masked with fragrance.

Quick-Dry Process

Our extraction method removes 95% of moisture during the cleaning process. Most sofas are ready for use within 2–4 hours, even in humid Kerala weather.

What We Clean

We clean all types of upholstered furniture — 2-seater and 3-seater sofas, L-shaped sectionals, recliners, ottomans, dining chairs and mattresses. We work on fabric, microfibre, velvet, suede, genuine leather and synthetic leather. If you're unsure whether your material is suitable, just call us — we'll advise you before booking.
