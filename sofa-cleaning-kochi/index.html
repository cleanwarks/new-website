Kochi's year-round humidity is a hidden enemy of sofas. Fabric upholstery absorbs moisture from the air, creating conditions where dust mites, allergens and odour-causing bacteria thrive deep within the padding — invisible on the surface. The monsoon season compounds this, bringing additional airborne moisture, mould spores and the fine dust that settles across Ernakulam district when the rains return.

Regular vacuuming removes surface dust but leaves embedded allergens, pet dander, food particles and bacterial colonies untouched. Clean Warks professional sofa cleaning uses hot steam extraction and targeted stain treatment to reach deep into the upholstery fibres, removing what vacuuming cannot — and leaving your sofa hygienically clean, not just visually tidier.

What's Included

Steam Extraction

Hot water extraction penetrates deep into fabric to remove embedded dust, allergens, bacteria and odour-causing particles.

Stain & Spill Treatment

Targeted stain removal for food, drink, ink, pet stains and general soiling using fabric-safe treatment agents.

Pet Hair Removal

Specialist tools remove embedded pet hair from fabric, microfibre and velvet upholstery.

Odour Elimination

Deodorising treatment neutralises musty smells and pet odours — especially important in Kochi's humid climate.

Leather Conditioning

For leather and faux-leather sofas: gentle clean followed by conditioning to prevent cracking.

Drying Assistance

We use industrial fans to speed drying time. Most sofas are usable within 3–5 hours.
