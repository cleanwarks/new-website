The Hidden Danger in Your Water Tank

Most households clean their home regularly — but forget the water tank. Over time, sediment, algae, rust particles, dead insects and bacterial biofilm accumulate at the bottom and along the walls of overhead tanks and underground sumps. This contaminated water then flows to your taps, your cooking pots and your drinking glasses.

Regular water tank cleaning is not just about hygiene — it's a public health requirement. Clean Warks provides professional tank cleaning with complete sediment removal, high-pressure wall scrubbing and chlorine disinfection, bringing your water storage back to safe standards.

Underground Sump Cleaning in Bangalore

In Bangalore, most apartment complexes and independent houses rely on underground sumps rather than overhead tanks alone. These sumps collect water from borewells and municipal supply before pumping it to overhead tanks — making sump hygiene critical for your entire building's water quality.

Clean Warks provides professional sump cleaning across Bangalore, including complete water extraction, high-pressure wall scrubbing, sludge removal and food-grade chlorination. Whether your sump is 1,000 litres or 50,000 litres, our team arrives with the right equipment. We serve individual apartments, gated communities, apartment associations and commercial buildings.

Common Sump Problems We Fix

  • Sediment and sludge accumulation at sump floor
  • Algae growth on sump walls (green/black deposits)
  • Bacterial biofilm causing water taste and odour issues
  • Rust particles from corroding inlet pipes
  • Insect entry through poorly sealed sump lids

What's Included in Every Tank Clean

Sediment & Sludge Removal

Water is pumped out and all accumulated sediment, sludge and debris at the tank floor is removed using suction equipment and manual tools.

Quarterly Plans Available

How Often Should Tanks Be Cleaned?

For a family home, cleaning your overhead tank every 3–6 months is recommended. Underground sumps may need more frequent attention depending on water source quality. During monsoon season, when water quality can be unpredictable, a post-monsoon tank clean is strongly advised.
