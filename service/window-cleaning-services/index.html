Clear Windows, Brighter Interiors

In Kerala's humid monsoon climate and Bangalore's dusty air, windows accumulate grime, mineral deposits, hard water spots and bird droppings surprisingly fast. Dirty windows don't just look bad — they reduce the amount of natural light entering your home or office, making interiors feel darker and smaller.

Clean Warks provides professional window and glass cleaning using pure water systems, squeegee techniques and professional glass cleaners that eliminate streaks and leave a crystal-clear finish. We clean both interior and exterior glass on all window types — including sliding windows, casement windows, fixed glass panels and glass façades.

What's Included in Every Window Clean

Interior & Exterior Glass

Both sides of all glass panels cleaned using professional squeegees and pure water or window cleaning solution — no streaks, no lint, no residue.

Window Track Cleaning

Sliding window channels vacuumed and wiped clean — removing trapped dirt, dead insects and moisture that cause stiff operation and corrosion over time.

Frame Wipe-Down

Aluminium, uPVC or wooden window frames wiped clean inside and out. Handles and locks wiped and checked for smooth operation.

Streak-Free Finish

We use lint-free microfibre cloths and professional window cleaning solution to achieve a perfectly clear, streak-free result on every pane.

High-Rise Safe Equipment

For upper-floor windows, we use telescopic water-fed poles and appropriate safety equipment — no need for ladders on standard residential heights.

Screen Cleaning

Insect mesh screens removed, gently brushed, washed and refitted — often overlooked but essential for clear, unobstructed window views.

Residential & Commercial Window Cleaning

We clean windows in apartments, independent houses, villas, offices, showrooms, hotels and retail spaces. For commercial properties with large glass façades or floor-to-ceiling glazing, we provide custom quotes based on area and access requirements.

Post-monsoon window cleaning is especially popular — after the rains, mineral deposits and watermarks from splash-back can make even new glass look old. Available across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore.
