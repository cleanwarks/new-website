Why It Matters

Why Your Sofa Needs More Than Vacuuming

🌫️

Dust goes deep

Kerala's humidity traps dust, pet dander and skin cells deep inside sofa fibres. Only deep shampoo cleaning reaches them.

🧪

Stains set permanently

Older stains become permanent without the right enzyme treatment. Professional cleaning removes stains that home methods can't.

🤧

Allergens cause health issues

Sofas are a major allergen source in homes. Regular professional shampoo cleaning significantly reduces dust mites, pet allergens and mould spores.
