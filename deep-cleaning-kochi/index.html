Kochi-Specific Deep Cleaning Challenges

Kochi's geography and climate create unique deep cleaning demands that general-purpose services often underestimate:

Post-monsoon mould. Kerala's monsoon (June–September) brings sustained humidity above 85%. Bathrooms, window grout and behind furniture commonly develop mould and mildew that needs professional-grade treatment.

Coastal salt deposits. Homes near Marine Drive, Fort Kochi and Willingdon Island accumulate salt deposits on window frames, grilles and tile surfaces that regular mopping cannot remove.

Post-construction dust. Kochi's rapid development means many apartments — especially in Kakkanad and Vyttila — have post-construction silica dust that needs HEPA vacuuming, not just mopping.
