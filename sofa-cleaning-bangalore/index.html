Why Bangalore Sofas Need Professional Cleaning

Bangalore's apartment lifestyle means sofas work harder than anywhere else. With ACs running year-round, dust and allergens circulate constantly and settle deep into upholstery fibres. Add the city's notorious dust from construction activity, pet hair from apartment-friendly breeds, and the food spills from WFH culture — and even a 6-month-old sofa can harbour more bacteria than a kitchen counter.

Clean Warks brings professional hot water extraction directly to your apartment in Koramangala, Bellandur, Whitefield, HSR Layout or wherever you are in Bangalore. No need to arrange transport — we arrive with all equipment and leave your sofa genuinely clean in 2–4 hours.

Sofa & Couch Cleaning for All Types of Furniture

Whether you call it a sofa or a couch, we clean them all. Our upholstery team is trained to handle every material and configuration — from compact 2-seaters to sprawling sectionals.

Fabric Sofas

Leather Sofas

Recliners

Sectional Sofas

What's Included in Every Sofa Clean

Hot Steam Extraction

High-temperature steam is injected into the upholstery under pressure, dislodging dirt, bacteria and allergens from deep within the fabric before they're vacuumed out.

Stain Treatment

Individual stains pre-treated with targeted stain removers before the main clean — effective on food, drink, ink, grease and biological stains.

Pet Hair Removal

Specialist rubber tools and strong suction used to remove pet hair embedded in fabric — including in seams, cushion edges and under cushions.

Fabric & Leather Safe

We assess your sofa material before starting — different products and methods are used for fabric, microfibre, velvet and genuine or faux leather.

Odour Elimination

Enzymatic deodorisers applied after cleaning to neutralise pet, food and musty odours at the source — not just masked with fragrance.

Quick-Dry Process

Our extraction method removes 95% of moisture during the cleaning process. Most sofas are ready for use within 2–4 hours.
