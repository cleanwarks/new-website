What Is a Deep Clean?

A deep clean goes far beyond a regular housekeeping visit. It's a methodical, top-to-bottom scrub of every surface, nook and corner in your home — including behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout lines, AC vents and window tracks that standard cleaning overlooks. It leaves your home genuinely hygienic, not just tidy-looking.

Deep cleaning is ideal when moving into a new home, preparing a property for handover, recovering from post-construction dust, or simply resetting your space after months of regular-only cleaning. Clean Warks teams use professional steam equipment, HEPA vacuums and hospital-grade disinfectants to tackle even the most stubborn buildup.

What's Included in a Deep Clean

Behind Appliances & Furniture

Refrigerator, washing machine, sofa and wardrobes moved and cleaned beneath and behind — where dust and grime accumulate unseen for months.

Inside Cabinets & Drawers

Kitchen and wardrobe cabinets emptied, interior surfaces wiped, and shelves degreased or dusted depending on usage.

Grout & Tile Scrubbing

Bathroom and kitchen tiles scrubbed with rotary brushes and acid-safe cleaners to remove black grout stains, limescale and soap scum.

AC Vent Cleaning

Split and window AC grilles, vents and accessible filters vacuumed and wiped — reducing dust circulation and improving air quality indoors.

Window Tracks & Frames

Sliding window channels, frames and sills vacuumed and wiped clean — a frequently ignored area that harbours insects and moisture.

Disinfection & Deodorising

High-touch surfaces disinfected with EPA-approved products. Rooms deodorised so your home smells as clean as it looks.

When Should You Book a Deep Clean?

We recommend a deep clean when moving into a new flat or villa, before a major celebration, after a renovation project, or at least once or twice a year as a thorough reset. If your home has been vacant for a while, or if you've noticed persistent odours or heavy dust buildup, a deep clean is the right starting point.
