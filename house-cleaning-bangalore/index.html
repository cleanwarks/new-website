Bangalore-Specific Cleaning Challenges

Our Bangalore teams are trained for the specific cleaning demands of the city's apartment communities:

Construction dust. Bangalore's relentless development means fine silica dust is a constant presence in apartments near construction sites. We use HEPA vacuum tools to capture fine particles that regular mopping spreads around.

Dry season dust. November to February brings dry, windy conditions that deposit dust across every surface faster than in monsoon-affected cities. Monthly cleaning is recommended during this period.

WFH culture. With Bangalore's large work-from-home population, homes are occupied more intensively. Our flexible scheduling accommodates bookings that don't disrupt your workday.
