Our Story

Built in Kochi. Trusted Across Kerala. Now in Bangalore.

Clean Warks was founded in Kochi in 2020 with a simple belief: professional cleaning should feel like hospitality, not a transaction. We started with a small team of trained technicians serving homes around Palarivattom — and word travelled fast.

By 2021 we'd opened our second hub in Kottayam. Today our trained crews serve four districts across Kerala — Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta and Alappuzha — and in 2024 we proudly took our standards to Bangalore.

The team, the processes, the eco-friendly products and the satisfaction guarantee — they're the same wherever you book us. That's the Clean Warks promise.
