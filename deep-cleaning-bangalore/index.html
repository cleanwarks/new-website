Bangalore's pace of development means thousands of new apartments are handed over to owners and tenants every month — often with post-construction dust in every crevice, builder residue on tiles and fittings, and the general grime of an unoccupied property. Move-in deep cleaning is Clean Warks' most requested Bangalore service, and our teams are specifically trained to handle the particular demands of newly built apartments in areas like Whitefield, Sarjapur Road, Bellandur and Electronic City.

Beyond move-ins, Bangalore's IT professional community has made the periodic deep clean a routine part of apartment maintenance. With many residents spending long hours at home (or working from home), and apartments in dense communities where dust and pollution filter in from outside, a thorough deep clean every 3–6 months keeps living spaces genuinely hygienic — not just surface-tidy.

What's Included

Behind Appliances & Furniture

Refrigerator, washing machine and heavy furniture moved; surfaces beneath cleaned and disinfected.

Grout & Tile Scrubbing

Bathroom and kitchen tiles scrubbed with rotary brushes — removing limescale, soap scum and mould.

Inside Cabinets & Drawers

All kitchen and wardrobe interiors emptied, wiped down and degreased.

AC Vents & Grilles

Split AC vents, grilles and filters vacuumed and wiped — reducing dust and allergen circulation.

Window Tracks & Frames

All sliding window channels, frames and sills vacuumed and cleaned.

Full Kitchen Degreasing

Hob, chimney, exhaust fans, backsplash tiles and surfaces — complete degreasing and sanitisation.

Most Popular Bangalore Deep Clean Scenarios

Move-in deep cleaning. Our most popular Bangalore service. We clean your new apartment before your furniture arrives so you start fresh. Covers post-construction dust, builder residue and sanitisation of all surfaces.

Move-out deep cleaning. Essential for tenants vacating apartments who need to recover their security deposit. We restore the property to a clean, presentable condition.

Post-renovation cleaning. After civil work, painting or carpentry — we remove construction dust, chemical residue and debris thoroughly.

Seasonal deep clean. IT professionals and WFH residents who want a thorough quarterly or bi-annual reset of their living spaces.
