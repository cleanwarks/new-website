First Impressions Start Outside

The exterior of your property is the first thing visitors, neighbours and guests see. Kerala's monsoon season leaves behind a layer of moss, mud stains, algae and watermarks on compound walls, driveways and terraces that can make even a well-maintained home look neglected. Regular outdoor cleaning protects your surfaces and preserves your property's value.

Clean Warks provides comprehensive outdoor and exterior cleaning using pressure washing equipment, surface scrubbers and eco-friendly cleaning agents. We restore driveways, terraces, exterior walls and garden paths to a clean, fresh appearance — safe for tile, concrete, brick and paint surfaces.

What's Included in Every Outdoor Clean

Compound & Driveway Wash

Paved compound and driveway surfaces washed with high-pressure equipment to remove mud, oil stains, algae and monsoon deposits from tiles, concrete and paving stones.

Terrace Cleaning

Roof terrace swept, debris cleared and surface washed. Drains cleared of leaves and silt. Waterproof coating inspected and standing water areas addressed.

Exterior Wall Scrubbing

Compound walls and building exterior walls scrubbed to remove moss, algae, mildew stains and watermarks — restoring original colour and preventing long-term damage.

Gate & Boundary Cleaning

Metal and wooden gates, fencing and boundary pillars cleaned of rust, algae and general exterior grime — helping preserve surface treatments and paint.

Drain Clearing

Outdoor drainage channels around the compound, driveway and terrace cleared of leaf litter, mud and debris to ensure proper water flow and prevent flooding.

Post-Monsoon Refresh

Comprehensive post-monsoon exterior package — compound wash, wall scrub, terrace clean and drain clear — to restore your property after the rains.

Keep Your Exterior Looking Its Best Year-Round

We offer one-time outdoor cleaning as well as biannual maintenance plans — ideal for a pre-monsoon prep and post-monsoon restore. For villas and larger properties, we can provide a custom outdoor cleaning package covering all exterior areas in a single visit.

Available across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore. Contact us for a site assessment and custom quote for large properties.
