Clean Office, Productive Team

A professionally cleaned office does more than look good — it reduces sick days, creates a positive impression on clients and boosts employee morale. Studies show employees are significantly more productive in a clean, organised workspace. Clean Warks provides reliable commercial cleaning on a schedule that works around your business, whether that's early morning, after hours or weekends.

We work with offices of all sizes — from small co-working desks to large corporate floors — as well as showrooms, retail outlets, clinics, restaurants and warehouses. Our trained teams arrive uniformed with all equipment and follow a consistent checklist so nothing is ever overlooked.

What's Included in Every Office Clean

Workstation & Desk Cleaning

Desks, keyboards, monitors and telephones wiped and sanitised. Chairs dusted. Filing cabinets and shelves wiped down. All done without disturbing paperwork or equipment.

Pantry & Kitchen

Office pantry counters wiped, microwave and fridge exteriors cleaned, sink scrubbed and appliances sanitised. Tea/coffee area cleaned after every visit.

Restroom Sanitisation

All toilets, urinals and washbasins scrubbed and disinfected. Mirrors cleaned, floors mopped, soap and paper dispensers restocked (supplies provided by client).

Floor Mopping & Vacuuming

All floor types cleaned — tiles mopped with disinfectant, carpeted areas vacuumed with HEPA-filter machines. Common corridors and reception areas prioritised.

Trash Removal

All waste bins emptied and relined. Confidential paper waste segregated if required. Recycling bins managed as per your office policy.

After-Hours Scheduling

We work around your business hours — early morning starts, evening cleans or weekend deep cleans available to ensure zero disruption to your team.

Flexible Plans for Every Business

We offer one-time cleans, daily, weekly and fortnightly maintenance contracts. For businesses requiring regular cleaning, a dedicated Clean Warks team is assigned to your premises for consistency and security. All commercial clients receive a service log with visit reports.

Available for offices, showrooms, clinics, restaurants, retail stores, co-working spaces and warehouses across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore. Contact us for a custom quote based on your floor area and cleaning frequency.
