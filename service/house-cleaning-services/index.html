What Does Our House Cleaning Cover?

A clean home is more than just tidied surfaces — it's a healthier, more comfortable space for you and your family. Clean Warks provides end-to-end house cleaning across all rooms, using professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets.

Our uniformed, background-verified technicians arrive on time with all equipment and supplies. We follow a systematic room-by-room checklist so nothing gets missed, whether it's a compact apartment or a large villa. Every job is supervised and quality-checked before we leave.

What's Included in Every Visit

Dusting All Surfaces & Ceiling Fans

Every shelf, ledge, ceiling fan blade and light fitting is dusted — including hard-to-reach spots that get neglected in daily cleaning.

Mopping & Vacuuming Floors

Floors swept, vacuum-cleaned and mopped with appropriate solutions for marble, tile, wood or vinyl surfaces.

Kitchen Wipe-Down

Counter surfaces, hob tops, sink, cabinet fronts and exterior of appliances wiped clean and degreased.

Bathroom Scrub

Toilet, sink, shower area, mirror and floor scrubbed and disinfected using hotel-grade hygiene products.

Bedroom & Living Area

Beds made, surfaces wiped, furniture dusted and floors cleaned. Living room sofas and cushions straightened.

Balcony Sweep

Balcony floor swept and mopped, railings wiped down — a finishing touch that completes the whole-home clean.

Why Choose Clean Warks for House Cleaning?

With over 5,000 homes cleaned since 2020, we've refined a process that delivers consistent results every time. Our technicians are trained, insured and courteous. We bring all products and equipment — you don't need to supply anything. If you're not satisfied, we'll come back and re-clean at no extra charge.

We offer flexible scheduling — morning, afternoon or evening slots — and one-time or recurring bookings (weekly, fortnightly, monthly). Available across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore.
