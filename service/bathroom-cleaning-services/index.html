Why Professional Bathroom Cleaning Matters

The bathroom is the most germ-prone room in the home, yet it's also the hardest to clean thoroughly. Limescale on taps, black grout lines, soap scum on shower glass, and persistent toilet stains require the right tools and products — not just an extra scrub. Clean Warks uses commercial-grade acid descalers, antibacterial disinfectants and professional steam to bring your bathroom to a truly hygienic standard.

Our technicians are trained on bathroom surfaces — marble, ceramic, porcelain and glass — so you can trust that the right product is used on every surface without risk of damage. We leave every bathroom sparkling, sanitised and smelling fresh.

What's Included in Every Bathroom Clean

Tile & Grout Deep Scrub

Wall and floor tiles scrubbed with rotary brushes. Grout lines treated with professional whitening and antibacterial agents to remove black mould and staining.

Toilet Disinfection

Toilet bowl, seat, cistern and base scrubbed and disinfected inside and out using hospital-grade bactericidal products that kill 99.9% of germs.

Shower & Bathtub Cleaning

Shower head descaled, glass panels de-scummed and bathtub scrubbed to remove soap residue, hard water stains and body-oil buildup.

Mirror & Glass Streak-Free Finish

Bathroom mirror and any glass panels polished to a streak-free shine using lint-free microfibre and professional glass cleaner.

Tap & Fixture Polishing

Chrome taps, towel rails, soap dispensers and other fittings descaled and polished to remove limescale and restore shine.

Floor Mopping & Drain Clearing

Floor mopped with disinfectant solution. Drain cleared of hair and debris and treated with drain cleaner to prevent slow drainage and odours.

How Often Should You Deep-Clean Your Bathrooms?

For a household in regular use, a professional bathroom clean every month keeps limescale and grout staining from building up. For rental properties, a full bathroom deep clean between tenancies is essential. We offer one-time bookings as well as monthly and fortnightly maintenance plans.

All work is carried out by trained, uniformed technicians using products that are safe for septic tanks and eco-friendly where possible. Available across Kochi, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha and Bangalore.
